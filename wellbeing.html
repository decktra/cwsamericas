Wellbeing

Progress in the lives of people according to what they value and have reasons to value, that is wellbeing. It is about the capacities, freedoms and opportunities that people need to achieve their own goals in life and flourish with prosperity and dignity in a better life. The domains or areas of life referred to are personal well-being, interpersonal and social relationships, good health, where you live, personal finances, economics, education and skills, government and civic life , the natural environment

Personal Wellbeing

Focused on the individual and the dynamics of his psychic life. Work to create higher levels of awareness about one's psychic life, taking into account the personal wellbeing and rituals of life that give it meaning.

Wellbeing and Workplace

Co-create with change agents to innovate in the field of quality of employment and the welfare of the worker. We design policies, practices and perform measurements on the changes experienced in the personal well-being of the worker, his family and the personal-work life balance.

Wellbeing, HD & Public Policies

Promote new approaches and models of social and business innovation that can be developed in models of collaboration and public-private partnership, in order to produce scalable and lasting impacts on Welfare issues.
