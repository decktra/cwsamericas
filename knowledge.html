Knowledge

Investigate collaboratively to activate new levels of knowledge of change agents about the social reality, its complexities and dynamics, about the individual, its psychology and well-being, in order to innovate on the practices and improve the collective impact produced by said actors in their different ecosystems

Collective Impacts

Work on the understanding and analysis of wellbeing as a multidimensional phenomenon by conducting rigorous research on dimensions not traditionally considered in the measurement and analysis of poverty. This to generate knowledge, establish relationships that better explain the phenomenon and assess its relevance to promote initiatives that aim to meet them.

We evaluate, monitoring and systematized the impacts of these initiatives under this well-being approach with the purpose of measuring the changes experienced in the population, learning and generating continuous improvements that allow to achieve lasting changes in people.

Thematic Research

Perform rigorous research on social, psychological, economic and political issues. Research specific issues that concern the individual, society or organizations to change agents. Research on issues related to poverty, welfare, psychology of the individual, business and sustainable development.
